1.1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects – How does GDPR impact digital projects?

GDPR is all about data, my data, your data, everyone’s data and how it can protect us. Data is collected and used for marketing purposes, to push targeted ads and to store our data profile like the police use fingerprints to identify people.

Companies use our data to identify and pursue us online. The GDPR act has helped prevent companies from storing this data and using it for profit by advertisements, giving users like you and me the option to opt out of these uses of our data. [1]
