Welcome To My Website!

Hello, I'm Ollie, thank you for visiting my page.

Here is a little something about me. I'm 38, I live in Stretford Manchester.

I like to brew beer, I brew with a couple of friends in Hulme, Manchester. We have been doing so since 2014.

My day job is a painter and decorator, I have done this since I left University, after gaining a Foundation Degree in Commercial Photography.

Also I have worked in the photography industry and gained some recognition through work and exhibitions.

I would like to break away from my current career as a painter and decorator and find a new path in my life. I discovered the course after someone recommended I try coding and someone from the company called me up. Many of my friends are coders and programmers and they say it is the future.

So far, my time on the course has been great. Highly informative and social. Both things that I really enjoy about learning and I think are a must.
