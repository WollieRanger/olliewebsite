1.1.2 Describing methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully – what do you need to make projects successful? What tools? What skills?

I use the Adobe series of programs, to do my editing, Adobe Bridge is the core of what I use as it’s a browser and I can organise everything from there, then with a click of a button I can be editing photographs in Photoshop or putting together a video in Premiere. I pay a monthly subscription to be able to use them. Without them, what I do for work and for creativity would not exist in the way that it does and to the standard I’d like. I pay a premium for the organisational aspects and the workflow advantages these programs give. There are no doubt free versions of equivalent programs, but with my experience these are not as good.
